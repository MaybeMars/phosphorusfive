Detailed Description

DNA code for Node, or its "Path" property.

The Path for any given Node is basically a "materializedd path" implementation, allowing you to partition your tree-structure, such that you can easily determine whether or not one specific node is "before" or "after" another node, and what types of relationship two nodes from the same tree have.
