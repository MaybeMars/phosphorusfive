The ActiveEvent attribute is used for marking your methods as Active Events. Declare an Active Event by adding this attribute to your class, and make sure it has a unique Name property. Optionally, make your Active Event override other existing Active Events by using Overrides. Active Events to a large extends replaces conventional functions and methods, and allows you to invoke methods by using the Raise method, and similar constructs. This allows you to completely loosely couple together your modules in your application, without having any dependencies between them at all, which facilitates for much more Agile software.

To raise an Active Event, use the Raise method. For instance;

_myContext.Raise ("foo.bar");
